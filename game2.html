Guess your age in hours!

How it works:

  1. The game will ask you how old you are on the first prompt simply enter your age.
  2. After you enter your age you will have 20 chances to correctly guess how many hours you've lived.
  3. If your guess is too low the game will inform "Your guess is too low, you should live longer."
  4. If your guess is too high the game will inform you "You haven't lived that long yet."
  5. If you guess more than 20 times you lose!
  6. Once you enter your age correctly in hours you've won!
